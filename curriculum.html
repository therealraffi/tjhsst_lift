STEM

A key aspect of LIFT that we love to implement is fostering the love for science in conjunction with the aspect of preparing for an admissions exam. If we can inspire middle schoolers to look forward to STEM-related activities, the admissions process will not only be easier, but it will also guide them to a new passion broader than just the scope of TJ. Thus, we host STEM activities to replace the one-on-one mentor sessions once a month. These activities range from hands-on building to logical games.
