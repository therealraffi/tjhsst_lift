Who Are We?

LIFT (Learning and Inquiry through Fellowship and Tutoring) is Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology's largest mentorship outreach program that identifies and supports high-achieving middle schoolers of minority backgrounds in order to prepare them for rigorous high school academics. We serve our mentees through tutoring sessions on Saturdays at TJ, one-on-one mentoring, and STEM enrichment opportunities.

"TJHSST LIFT has changed the lives of hundres of aspiring thinkers. I say with pride that we, as a community are making changed"

Jay Wickliff, Coordinator

We make learning fun!

Monthly enrichment activities

Every month we have an enriching, STEM-based activity to engage our students’ minds beyond the classroom. These include construction challenges, experiments, and other fun, teamwork-centered explorations. Online classes will limit the kinds of activities we do, but it won’t limit our curiosity! LIFT will work to make sure that every activity is accessible for each student regardless of the home situation.

Completely Free!

Learn without worrying

Every LIFT session is absolutely free because we’re dedicated to making education and TJ accessible for all, regardless of economic or social status. We also have free pizza, snacks, and drinks every Saturday!
