Who Are We?

LIFT (Learning and Inquiry through Fellowship and Tutoring) is Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology's largest mentorship outreach program that identifies and supports high-achieving middle schoolers of minority backgrounds in order to prepare them for rigorous high school academics. We serve our mentees through tutoring sessions on Saturdays at TJ, one-on-one mentoring, and STEM enrichment opportunities.

"TJHSST LIFT has changed the lives of hundreds of aspiring thinkers. I say with pride that we, as a community are making a change."

Jay Wickliff, Former Coordinator

Mentors and Teachers

LIFT boasts 100 mentors, all of whom are TJ students. Our mentors have a unique perspective on TJ’s admissions process as they have been through it themselves and are highly qualified to guide students through TJ admissions and life at TJ.

TJ Admissions Change

FCPS has recently announced that the original TJ test is likely gone. LIFT will still be geared towards tutoring our mentees, but we will be less focused on standardized testing.

Distance Learning Plan

Just like FCPS, LIFT has also adapted to the remote-learning system. We will be having our meetings online on either Mondays or Wednesdays after school (TBD) and will either use Google Meets or Blackboard Collaborate to host the meetings.

We make learning fun!

Monthly enrichment activities

Every month we have an enriching, STEM-based activity to engage our students’ minds beyond the classroom. These include construction challenges, experiments, and other fun, teamwork-centered explorations. Online classes will limit the kinds of activities we do, but it won’t limit our curiosity! LIFT will work to make sure that every activity is accessible for each student regardless of their home situation.

Completely Free!

Learn without worrying

Every LIFT session is absolutely free because we’re dedicated to making education at TJ accessible to all, regardless of economic or social status. We also have free pizza, snacks, and drinks every Saturday!
