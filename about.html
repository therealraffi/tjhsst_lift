Our Mission

Our goal is to serve our middle school students and encourage and prepare them to take rigorous high school academics. We strive to provide them with the resources to succeed academically and develop a curiosity towards STEM or other fields of their choosing.

"LIFT is an organization that works year in and year out to promote diversity through exposing underrepresented groups at TJ at a young age to STEM. I am thankful to have been able to be a part of this wonderful organization and seeing many students, mentors and mentees including myself, grow every week."

Abeer Zaib,
Former Mentor Leader

"TJHSST LIFT has changed the lives of hundres of aspiring thinkers. I say with pride that we, as a community are making changed"
